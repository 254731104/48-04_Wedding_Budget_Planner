Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Wedding Budget Planner. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website and tools.

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data. Our budget calculator and expense tracker run entirely in your browser. We do not store your budget data on our servers. Any data you enter is stored locally in your browser's localStorage.

We may collect anonymous analytics data (page views, browser type, device type) through Google Analytics to improve our website experience.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect: names, email addresses, physical addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information through our budget tools. No registration or account creation is required.

4. Cookies

We use minimal cookies: only a localStorage flag to remember your dark/light mode preference. Third-party services (Google Analytics, Google AdSense) may set their own cookies as described in their respective privacy policies.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party services including Google AdSense for advertising. These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them. We do not share any data with advertisers.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at our contact page.
