About Wedding Budget Planner

Our Mission

Planning a wedding should be one of the most joyful experiences of your life, not a source of financial stress. That's why we created Wedding Budget Planner & Tracker -- a free, simple, and transparent tool designed to help you estimate, plan, and track every dollar of your wedding budget.

We believe that every couple deserves access to professional-quality budget planning tools, regardless of their budget size or wedding style. Whether you're planning a small elopement with 10 guests or a grand celebration with 200 attendees, our tools adapt to your needs.

Why We're Different

Unlike popular platforms like TheKnot, Zola, and WeddingWire, we've built our tool with a fundamentally different philosophy: free first, always.

  • No registration required. Start planning immediately without creating an account or sharing personal information.
  • No vendor commissions. Our budget estimates aren't biased toward any particular vendor or service provider. We give you unbiased benchmarks based on industry data.
  • No paid upgrades. Every feature is 100% free, forever. There are no premium tiers, no hidden fees, and no upsells.
  • No bloat. Clean, focused tools that load fast and work on any device. No social media distractions or vendor push notifications.

What We Offer

Budget Calculator

Select your wedding size, location, and style to get an instant customized budget estimate with category breakdowns.

Expense Tracker

Input your planned budget and actual spending for each category. Automatically track deviations and get overspending alerts.

Industry Benchmarks

See how your spending compares to industry standards. Know what percentage each category should realistically consume.

City Cost Comparison

Compare average wedding costs across NYC, LA, Chicago, and Seattle to understand your local market.

Who We Are

We're a small team of product designers, developers, and wedding enthusiasts who were frustrated by the complexity and hidden costs of existing wedding planning platforms. After attending what felt like our 20th wedding planning appointment -- where every "free" tool started asking for credit card info -- we decided to build something better.

Our team brings experience from the consumer productivity and finance tooling space. We've spent hundreds of hours building the most intuitive, fastest-loading wedding budget calculator on the web.

Our Promise

We promise to keep this tool simple, fast, and free. We will never sell your data, bombard you with vendor promotions, or hide features behind paywalls. If you find a better free wedding budget tool, we'd love to hear about it -- but we're pretty confident you won't.

Got feedback or a feature idea? Reach out to us. We read every message and appreciate every suggestion.
